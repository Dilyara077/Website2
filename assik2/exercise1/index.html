Princess Diana

About Diana

Princess Diana (1961–1997), also known as the "People's Princess", was a member of the British royal family. She was admired worldwide for her compassion, charity work, and sense of style.

Key Life Events

  1. 1961 – Born in Sandringham, England
  2. 1981 – Married Prince Charles
  3. 1982 – Birth of Prince William
  4. 1984 – Birth of Prince Harry
  5. 1997 – Died in Paris, France

Major Contributions

  • Advocacy for HIV/AIDS awareness
  • Support for landmine removal campaigns
  • Work with children's hospitals
  • Global humanitarian efforts
